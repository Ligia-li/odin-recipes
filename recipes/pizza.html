Pizza

Description

First we are aking the dough and for that we use yeast and sugar and water, we let it to rise a bit, then we add flour till it becomes thick and we can not stir it anymore with the fork. After that we let it to rise. Secondly we prepare the ingredients that we want to have on the dough. So we start cutting some pepperoni, some salami, some peppers and some olives, and for sure some mozarella for the crust. After we have all the ingredents done and the dough has rise enough we turn dough onto a floured surface and pat or roll into 12 inch circle. We mix the ingredients, or we put them how we want and after that we put t in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast

  • 2 Â½ cups bread flour

Steps for the recipe

  1. Start by making the dough
  2. Let the dough rise and after that let it rest
  3. Cut the ingredients
  4. Spread the dough on a clean surface
  5. Put the ingredients on
  6. Put it in the oven and let it for 15-20 minutes
  7. Take it from the oven
  8. Enjoy! :D
